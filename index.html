Graphic Designer & Visual Storyteller

I’m currently a senior at Millersville University working on my Bachelor’s in Interactive and Graphic Design.

As someone who prefers to do multiple things rather than one, I enjoy experimenting with different programs, styles, and techniques to learn and further develop my design skills. Whether it is developing a brand identity or user experience, I’m committed to continuous learning and finding original ways to tell stories through design.
